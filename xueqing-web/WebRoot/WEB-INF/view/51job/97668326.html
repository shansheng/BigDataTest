职位信息

职位描述：
技术支持工程师将负责在杭州落地的特定投资公司的业务发展活动，其中包括销售和营销活动。这个人还将具有技术和开发工程师的支持作用，与杭州办事处和多伦多办事处的其他业务开发和运营人员合作，因此这个角色可能适合有经验的业务发展技术专业人员，有兴趣维护技术角色或寻求获得业务发展经验的技术支持工程师.

Job Description:
The Technical Support Engineer will be tasked with responsibilities of technical sales support activities for specific portfolio companies landed in Hangzhou, which will include sales and marketing activities. This person will additionally also have a technical and development engineer support role, working with other business development and operations personnel in both the Hangzhou office and Toronto office, and therefore this role may suit either an experienced business development technical professional with an interest in maintaining a technical role, or a technical support engineer looking to gain business development experience.

典型职责：
? 研究，诊断，排除故障并确定解决方案，以解决系统问题，包括帐户设置和网络配置
? 按照标准程序将未解决的问题适当分配到内部团队
? 通过电话，电子邮件或聊天等一系列行动与客户交谈，直到他们解决了技术问题
? 可以在同一情况下优先处理和管理几个未决问题
? 跟进客户，确保故障排除后，IT系统功能齐全
? 向客户提供及时准确的反馈
? 确保所有问题都正确记录
? 准备准确和及时的报告
? 以笔记和手册的形式记录技术知识
? 与现有客户联系并保持关系
? 提供售前和售后支持
? 提供产品教育和咨询
? 必要时参加贸易展览和会议
? 管理层分配的其他任务
Typical responsibilities:
? Actively identify and establish new business.
? Taking ownership of customer issues reported and seeing problems through to resolution
? Researching, diagnosing, troubleshooting and identifying solutions to resolve system issues including account setup and network configuration
? Following standard procedures for proper escalation of unresolved issues to the appropriate internal teams
? Talk clients through a series of actions, either via phone, email or chat, until they’ve solved a technical issue
? Prioritize and manage several open issues at one time
? Follow up with clients to ensure their IT systems are fully functional after troubleshooting
? Provide prompt and accurate feedback to customers
? Ensure all issues are properly logged
? Prepare accurate and timely reports
? Document technical knowledge in the form of notes and manuals
? Liaise with existing clients and maintain relationships.
? Provide pre-sales and after-sales support.
? Provide product education and advice.
? Attend trade exhibitions, conferences and meetings where required.
? Other tasks assigned by the management

工作条件：
旅行：国际旅行和培训的机会（约1个月/年）
工作时间：主要是星期一到星期五，由于北美和中国的时差，上班时间不一

Work conditions:
Travel: opportunities for international travel and training (approximately 1 month/year)
Working hours: mainly Monday to Friday, with frequent after work hours due to the time difference between North America and China.

知识，技能和能力：
? 卓越的沟通能力
? 能够有效地协调和同时处理多项工作，并与客户建立良好的关系
? 能够通过培训和工作经验掌握快速变化的技术和应用的专业知识
? 能够与代表、客户和公司人员建立和保持良好的工作关系，具有较强的人际关系技巧
? 工作主动性强，能够快速学习和独立完成工作
? 较强的组织和解决问题技能
? 注意细节和准确性
? 较强的打字、语法、 写作和校对技能，用于撰写和编辑商务信函和商务计划（包括普通话和英语）
? 优秀的数据分析能力

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
? Effective communication skills.
? Ability to coordinate multiple jobs and build good relationship with the clients
? Ability to acquire through training and on the job experience specialized knowledge in rapidly changing technology and applications.
? Strong communication and human relation skills to develop and maintain positive working relationships with representatives, clients, and company personnel
? Excellent initiative, ability to learn quickly and work independently
? Strong organizational and problem solving skills
? Attention to detail and accuracy
? Strong typing, grammar, spelling, writing, and proofreading skills for composing and editing business correspondence and business plans in both Mandarin and English
? Exceptional quantitative and analytical skills

资格：
理想的候选人将成立技术支持工程师，对以下技术具有一定理解：
? 拥有技术支持工程师，桌面支持工程师，IT帮助台技术人员或类似角色具有丰富的工作经验
? 拥有Windows / Linux / Mac OS环境的实践经验
? 熟悉电脑系统，行动装置及其他科技产品
? 熟悉远程桌面应用程序，运动可穿戴应用程序和云计算应用程序
? 具有计算机科学／工程、体育或软件工程学士学位的学士学位将会被优先考虑

Qualifications:
? Ideal candidates will be established Technical Support Engineer with a component level understanding of some of the following:
? Proven work experience as a Technical Support Engineer, Desktop Support Engineer, IT Help Desk Technician or similar role
? Hands-on experience with Windows/Linux/Mac OS environments
? Good understanding of computer systems, mobile devices and other tech products
? Familiarity with remote desktop applications, sports wearable applications and cloud computing applications

待遇：面议，根据经验考虑
Compensation: Negotiable and subject to experience.

职能类别： 售前/售后技术支持工程师 业务拓展主管/经理
