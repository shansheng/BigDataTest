职位信息

关于Cloudaxis
Cloudaxis是一群具有团队精神的技术团队，他们将在中国香港和中国南方的云计算市场上玩得很开心。如果你有想法，有创造力，对你所做的事情充满激情，并且有目标导向，你应该成为我们团队的一员。

你要做什么（PS：有较好的英语读写能力）
——有效地了解客户需求,与内部团队合作,设计解决方案,以满足他们的需求。
——编写测试计划和测试用例
——测试iOS和Android应用
——测试节点JS后端应用程序
——执行分配的任务，及时维护应用程序的健康
——识别机会,减少重复工作,实际(和/或提高性能、降低风险等)
——清楚地与团队成员、客户和领导沟通

需求
——对相关技术平台有很好的理解(SQL / PL / SQL,Oracle)
——定义/维护使用性能/监控工具的***实践
——计划,编写和执行测试
——协助项目团队测试故障，诊断问题
——强大的移动应用技术的理解
——Java技术的基本理解
——英语良好优先

About Cloudaxis
Cloudaxis is a team-spirited group of technologists that is going to have fun taking over the cloud computing market in Hong Kong and Southern China. If you have ideas, are creative, feel passionate about what you do and are goal-oriented, you should be part of our team.

What you’ll be doing
? Testing the work of the web and mobile development team.
? Collaborating with your team in an Agile environment.
? Creating test plans based on the given specifications
? Validating the code as per the functional requirements
? Using Selenium to automate the tests.

Requirements
? 2+ years of experience testing enterprise grade applications
? Experience using Selenium or other automated test framework.
? Experience using a tool sich as Jira or Bugzilla to track issues
? Knowledge of Maven and Continuous Integration would be a plus
? Previous experience using Agile Methodologies would be a plus
? Ability to communicate in english
? Degree in Computer Science or Engineering
