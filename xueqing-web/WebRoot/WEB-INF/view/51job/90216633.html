职位信息

Calix Inc. China Development Center is recruiting a Principal Engineer who will be responsible for Data Collecting in Cloud Service projects.

To apply this job, please visit:
https://jobs-calix.icims.com/jobs/4161/principal-software-engineer---data-collection/job

Responsibilities:
? You will play a vital role in the development and maintenance of a unified network management system of a multi-services telecom access platform.
? You will be involved in the development and maintenance of a large-scale, Massive Data analysis system in cloud environment.
? You will take responsibility to develop and maintain our cloud core module, netflow data collector.
? You will work with our cross function teams to make sure our cloud strategy well applied
? You will participate in investigating advanced deployment technology and applying it into Calix Cloud services.
? You will participate in design, implementation, and maintenance activities of large-scale cloud based data analysis system.

Qualifications:
? Network communication and general understanding of IP networks
? Understanding of Linux ways of dealing with networking
? Understanding of Linux multiprocessing / multithreading, scheduling
? Linux process / threads synchronization
? Be familiar with Netflow protocol
? Rich experience in C++ and Python multi-threads based network programming,
? Good understanding of C++ templates
? Understanding of space (memory) and time (CPU) optimization technics / algorithms
- hash maps
- data packing
- 32 / 64 bit architectures
- caching
? Experience with MongoDB
? Experience with messaging systems such as Kafka
? Reasonable understanding of dynamic and statically typed / compiled languages will be a plus. Specifically:
- Cython
- JavaScript
? Be familiar with Java will be a plus
? Strong problem solving skill
? Strong interest and ability to learn new skills/technologies

职能类别： 互联网软件开发工程师 高级软件工程师

关键字： Netflow 数据收集, data collection cython hash map

公司信息

Calix Inc. (“Calix”) 由数名通信行业的先行者创立于1999年，是全球最大的专注于接入方案的通信设备和服务供应商，其在北美，欧洲和亚洲有八个分支机构，近1000名员工。Calix总部位于加利福尼亚州佩塔卢马，另有六大研发中心，分别位于：波士顿，圣芭芭拉，硅谷，明尼阿波利斯，德克萨斯和南京。

长久以来，Calix始终专注于为通讯服务供应商提供一流综合解决方案。Calix研发的创新性接入平台和管理系统， 高度的集成和健全的服务使得服务供应商能够以最优的成本组合在任何类型的接入设备上提供任何服务。不仅如此，Calix还拥有反应迅捷，高度整合的供应链，能够在较短时间内大规模交付优质产品。同时，大量的免费服务和支撑功能极大地简化了培训、故障解决和网络升级，从而使得Calix的客户在服务价格、服务质量和业务规模上都是行业翘楚，尽享竞争优势。Calix致力于下一代的网络架构，推动软件定义网络SDN和网络功能虚拟化NFV，逐步把服务和管理都部署在云端，更快更灵活地发布新的业务，全天候满足客户需求。

2010年9月，Calix中国研发中心——“凯易讯”正式成立，位于南京软件谷。中国研发中心是Calix全球发展最快最稳健的研发中心，包含软硬件开发和测试，客户支持，项目管理等诸多部门，参与公司核心项目的研发工作。中国研发中心现有员工近300名。绝大多数员工都来自于国内外著名高校，并且有在业内顶尖公司的从业经历。
