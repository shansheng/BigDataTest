职位信息

Job Description:
职位描述：

The incumbent is responsible for managing IT service management processes and program/projects to serve the technology needs of the business unites in China. He/she will lead a team to work across the entire China GT to achieve the synergies from process and project perspective across various GT tracks. The position is based in China, Shanghai.
任职者负责管理中国IT服务管理流程和IT项目管理的业务需求，将领导团队实现跨各IT技术团队的协同工作，包括服务流程和项目管理的协同。该职位工作地点在中国，上海。

？ Manage SBU and Function stakeholders, act as point of contact to front the business and functional requirements from China business and functional unites， understand business and functional strategy and requirements, and translate into IT initiatives.
管理各事业部和职能部门的业务干系人，联络并了解中国各业务部门和职能部门的需求，理解并转化业务战略和需求为IT服务技术与项目计划。

？ Responsible for implementation, improvement and compliance of Project Management methodologies and processes，and represent from CN GT to liaise with Global GT.
负责IT项目管理方法论和流程的合理优化并落地执行，代表中国IT与全球团队进行协同工作。

？ Overall responsibility for IT project delivery across China, lead project delivery team from initiation to execution to closure. Aggregate projects into program level to achieve synergies across projects, functions and regions. Overall performance responsibility for managing project scope, quality, cost, schedule, which includes applying techniques for planning, tracking, change control, and risk management. Monitor program/project performance, progress and escalations, Ensure the smooth transition from project implementation to service operation.
全面负责中国所有IT项目的执行交付，领导项目团队完成项目启动到成功收尾。合理聚合项目进行项目群级别的管理，以实现跨项目/职能/地区的协合作用。全面负责项目范围、质量、成本、进度的管理，包括应用计划、跟踪、变更控制和风险管理等技术。监控所有项目的绩效、进展和升级。确保项目顺利实施并交付运维。

？ Responsible for managing all project resources in the Matrix organization, including external vendors, and for establishing an effective communication plan with the project team and the business stakeholders.
负责管理矩阵组织内的所有项目资源，包括外部供应商，并与项目组和业务干系人建立有效的沟通计划。

？ Lead the aggregation of department financial report, and coordinate various IT teams to ensure healthy budgeting, tracking and reporting, including project budget and operation budget.
领导IT部门的财务汇报，协调各IT团队进行预算，跟踪和汇报工作，包括项目预算和运营预算。

？ Participate and perform possible solution analysis/design/elicitation for pre-project system concept evaluation / assessment. Assist to develop business case to justify technology investments. Participate and coordinate the project governance (registration and approval) and represent from CN GT to liaise with Global GT.
参与执行对项目前期的概念评估进行可行性方案分析、设计、引导。协助团队制定商业计划书以证明技术投资和商业价值。参与并协调项目治理（立项与审批），代表中国IT与全球团队协同工作。

？ Responsible for implementation, improvement and compliance of ITSM/ITIL processes (incident, problem, change, etc.) and represent from CN GT to liaise with Global GT. Monitor the process performance and service delivery possibility.
负责IT服务管理流程的合理优化并落地执行，包括突发事件、问题和变更管理等流程，作为中国IT的代表与全球团队进行协同工作。

？ Maintain satisfactory working relationships with team members, vendors and clients as required to successfully achieve assigned tasks
与团队成员、供应商和客户保持良好的工作关系，成功完成分配的任务

？ Possess a strong willingness to learn on the job continuously, embracing new and varied technologies that are centered around all aspects of information technology.
具有持续学习工作的强烈意愿，接受以信息技术为中心的各种新技术。

？ To succeed in this role, strong communication skills, can-do attitude, result driven and willingness to perform hands-on work is necessary
有很强的沟通能力、不怕困难勇于挑战的、结果驱动、并愿意从事实际工作。

Desirable skills & Experience
？ 10+ years hands-on working experience in the field of information technology management. Must have experiences of project management and/or service delivery management. Solution design, development, implementation, technical support knowledge and experience is a plus.
10年以上IT领域管理实践经验。必须具备项目管理或服务交付经验。具有解决方案设计、开发、实施、技术支持知识和经验者优先。

？ 5+ years project management/coordination experience in information technology, infrastructure or application projects, and property opening.
5年以上IT基础设施或应用开发或物业开业等项目的管理/协调经验。

？ 5+ years of enterprise level service delivery of complex enterprise IT environment.
5年以上复杂企业IT环境的运维和服务交付。

？ Deep knowledge in Large-Scale enterprise system design/development/implementation/support.
熟悉大型企业系统的设计/开发/实施/支持。

？ Sufficient competency in one or more of the following technology areas: Active Directory, Windows, Network, System, Cloud computing, Unified Communication Collaboration, Software Defined Data Center, Enterprise applications.
对以下一个或多个领域有足够能力：域管理，操作系统，网络，系统，云计算，统一通信和协同，软件定义数据中心，企业级应用程序。

？ General understanding of business operations (such as IT solution development for retail/real estate/hospitality industry)
熟悉商业运作（如零售/房地产/酒店业IT解决方案开发）。

？ Strong sense of responsibility and Teamwork player
较强的责任心和团队合作精神。

？ Strong skill of communication.
较强的沟通能力。

？ Degree in Computer Science or Information Technology or equivalent.
计算机科学或信息技术或相关专业。

职能类别： 系统工程师

公司信息

【凯德集团】
凯德集团是亚洲规模最大的房地产集团之一，总部设在新加坡，并在新加坡上市。集团的房地产和房地产基金管理业务集中于核心市场新加坡和中国。
集团的房地产业务多元化，包括商业综合体、购物商场、服务公寓、办公楼和住宅；也同时拥有亚洲规模最大的房地产基金管理业务之一。凯德集团凭借其雄厚资产、多元房地产领域知识、先进产品设计和开发技能、积极资本管理策略以及庞大市场网络，持续开发优质房地产产品与服务。
凯德集团旗下的上市房地产投资信托基金包括雅诗阁公寓信托、凯德商务产业信托、凯德商用新加坡信托、凯德商用马来西亚信托，和凯德商用中国信托。

凯德中国 CLC
凯德中国是凯德集团在华全资子公司，是中国领先的房地产外企。以上海为起点，20年来凯德中国已经成长为全国性的地产企业。核心业务涵盖住宅、办公楼、来福士综合体及房地产金融,。总开发规模达到1,600万平方米，同时管理着7支注资中国项目的房地产基金。
凯德中国在房地产投资、开发、资产管理和房地产金融领域拥有核心竞争力，通过自身完整的房地产价值链，提供优质的房地产产品及服务。公司拥有8座“来福士”综合体旗舰项目，均为所在城市设计独树一帜的地标性建筑，为城市居民带来不同凡响的居住、工作和购物综合体验。
“树人建宇”是凯德的企业信条。凯德中国致力于员工发展，旨在营建积极的工作环境，为员工创造有效的发展平台；同时恪守企业社会责任，热心社会公益事业，为创造一个和谐人性的社会空间而不断努力。
